# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

Regardless of:

* Experience level
* Nationality
* Gender identity
* Personal background
* Technical expertise

All contributors deserve respect.

---

## Expected Behavior

Examples of positive behavior:

* Being respectful
* Accepting constructive criticism
* Sharing knowledge
* Helping newcomers
* Collaborating professionally

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples include:

* Harassment
* Personal attacks
* Discrimination
* Trolling
* Threats
* Offensive language

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, discussions or contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

Repeated violations may result in temporary or permanent bans from project participation.
